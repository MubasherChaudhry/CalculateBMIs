Coding Exercise #1

Mack and Mobi are trying to compare their BMI (Body Mass Index), which is calculated using the formula: BMI = mass / height ** 2 = mass / (height * height) (mass in kg and height in meter).

Tasks to do

  • Store Mack's and Mobi's mass and height in variables
  • Calculate both their BMIs using the formula (you can even implement both versions)
  • Create a Boolean variable 'MackHigherBMI' containing information about whether Mack has a higher BMI than Mobi.

Test Data

  • Data 1: Mack weights 78 kg and is 1.69 m tall. Mobi weights 92 kg and is 1.95 m tall.
  • Data 2: Mack weights 95 kg and is 1.88 m tall. Mobi weights 85 kg and is 1.76 m tall.
